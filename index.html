Who we are, what we do...

Coral's Surf Shack is based at the Cornich in Red Sea Mall in Jeddah on the west coast of Saudi Arabia and provides a friendly, safe and fun introduction to the exhilarating art of surfing.

Our expert crew of surf-dude instructors invite you and your friends to come and experience the thrill and excitement of surfing at Sudi Arabia’s best kept secret. SJA started Coral's Surf Shack out of a desire to live on the west coast and to share the thrill of surfing. You are very welcome to Coral's Surf Shack where surfing classes and surf lessons come highly recommended.

Services

We have Saudi Arabia’s biggest range of surf school surfboards. From 10 foot monster boards, that make surfing easy for taller/stronger perople, to 6 foot short boards, we have you covered. No matter what your surfing ability or experience, our aim is to make surfing easy for you. That’s when it gets fun!

Equipment Rental

Surf Rentals are available at Coral's Surf Shack. We have a full range of BIC hard boards and 1 and 3 fin soft boards. Rental sessions are based on two hours and rentals are made at the Red Sea Mall near Cornich. All rentals have to remain in Fanore Beach.

To rent you need to be a good swimmer, an advanced or intermediate surfer and be over 18 years of age (for insurance reasons). Conditions vary so much in Fanore, so on choppy days we only rent to advanced level surfers. No matter what level you are at you can always learn so much in a two hour surf lesson, it’s safer and usually easier for yourself. Thus we do whenever possible recommend a surf lesson.

Surfing Lessons

You will learn how to perfect your “POP UP” to your feet in 1,2 or 3 steps!

Equipment is provided along with one of our fully qualified Irish Surfing Association Surf instructors who will patiently take you through the complete surf experience. Lessons are held in waist deep water so you don’t need to be an amazing swimmer. Each Coral's Surf Shack lesson is 2 hours long, 80-90 minutes of which is spent in the water.
